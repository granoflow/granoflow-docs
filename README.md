## 2. Why Try It Now

You do not need to read everything first. We strongly recommend trying Granoflow in your real scenarios first.

Common trial scenarios:
- You open many tools every day: to-dos in chat, ideas in notes, plans in your calendar, and then suddenly feel like doing none of them.
- You have a project that takes several weeks, not one day. You work late every day, but tasks still slip past their deadlines, and the anxiety keeps building.
- You are tired of reacting to urgent interruptions. By the time you return to the task, you have already forgotten where you stopped.
- You are not trying to pack every day full. You want a rhythm you can keep returning to across work, study, health, and creative work.
- You are willing to try a product that is still moving quickly, and send us the real problems, stuck points, and improvement ideas you encounter.

> Product details are already documented in the first chapters of the manual, so this README does not duplicate that content.

## 3. Trial Incentive Program (Free Annual Membership)

We place real user feedback at the center of product iteration.

### 3.1 Reward Type
- **Annual membership** (granted by rules)

### 3.2 How to Qualify
You can qualify via any one of the following:

1. **Submit a valid issue**  
   - Search existing issues first to avoid duplicates.
   - For the same problem, only the first valid reporter is rewarded.
   - Example: reproducible bug, clear abnormal behavior, actionable clues.

2. **Submit a suggestion that gets adopted**  
   - Search existing issues first to avoid duplicate suggestions.
   - For the same direction, only the first adopted valid suggestion is rewarded.
   - Example: suggestion with clear scenario, objective, and value.

3. **Submit a high-quality experience-sharing post**  
   - Publishing platform must be either:
     - publicly viewable without login; or
     - a global mainstream social platform (e.g. X/Twitter, YouTube, Reddit, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, Zhihu, Weibo).
   - You must provide a direct link to the original post, and it must still be accessible 24 hours after publishing.
   - Content requirements:
     - centered on your own real work/life/practice experience, not promotional copy;
     - naturally mention one specific way Granoflow helped you;
     - include at least a minimal "problem → approach → outcome" loop.

### 3.3 Evaluation & Delivery
- "valid", "adopted", and "substantial" are judged by maintainers based on quality and practical value.
- Repeated submissions, missing key info, or obvious low-quality spam are not rewarded.
- Issue rewards follow the "first valid report" rule.
- Suggestion rewards follow the "first adopted suggestion" rule.
- Reward process and timeline will be announced publicly in repository channels.

### 3.4 Where to Publish
- Issue reports and feature suggestions: GitHub Issues  
  <https://github.com/granoflow/granoflow-docs/issues>
- Experience sharing: any publicly verifiable platform that meets the rules in section 3.2.

## 5. What We Want Most

### 5.1 Bug Report
- Environment (device / OS / version)
- Reproduction steps
- Expected result vs actual result
- Screenshots or recordings (if available)

### 5.2 Feature Suggestion
- Specific scenario to solve
- Current workaround and limitations
- Expected outcome (preferably measurable)

### 5.3 Experience Sharing
- Share your real work/life/practice context first (not marketing copy)
- Explain one specific point where Granoflow helped
- Describe the change with "problem → approach → outcome"
- Provide direct post link and ensure it remains accessible after 24 hours

## 8. FAQ (Growing)

- Who can join this incentive program?
- Can one person receive rewards multiple times?
- How long does confirmation usually take?
- How is annual membership delivered?
- What counts as a valid “experience sharing” post?
  - Real work/life/practice experience, not promotional copy.
  - Clear "problem → approach → outcome" loop with concrete details.
  - Naturally mentions one specific help point from Granoflow.
  - Includes direct post link and remains accessible after 24 hours.
- What is considered invalid “experience sharing”?
  - Pure advertising style, slogan-like promotion, no real personal context.
  - Vague content with no clear problem/approach/outcome.
  - Homepage/profile links only, without direct post link.
  - Deleted/private/inaccessible within 24 hours.
