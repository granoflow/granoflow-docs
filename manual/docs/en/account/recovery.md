title: "Account recovery"
description: "Submit an account recovery request to help verify whether a store purchase can be connected to your GranoFlow account."
---

Account recovery solves one specific problem: **you purchased GranoFlow in the App Store or Google Play, but you are not sure which account that purchase is connected to**.

It is not an entry point for account deletion, sign-out, subscription restore, or data recovery.

![Account recovery screen](../../../screenshots/en/account-recovery-main.png)

## When to use account recovery

✅ Appropriate:

- You are certain you purchased GranoFlow but cannot see member benefits in your current account
- You are not sure which email you used when you registered

❌ Not appropriate:

- You just want to restore the current platform purchase → use "Restore purchase"
- Your local data is missing → use "Backup and restore"
- You need to recover your encryption key → use "Encryption and recovery key"

## Steps

1. Find the account recovery link on the sign-in page or account page
2. Enter the email address you want to connect
3. Confirm this device can access the store account used for the original purchase
4. Submit the request and wait for the system to verify

## Possible outcomes

| Result | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Request submitted | System received the request; follow the prompts or check your email |
| No history found | No eligible purchase found under the current platform or account |
| Records do not match | Store record and email address cannot be connected through current verification |
| Temporary failure | Network or service issue; try again later |

:::caution[Do not delete data yet]
If you suspect you are signed in to the wrong account, **do not delete local data first**. Confirm your current signed-in email, then check the subscription page and device management.
:::
