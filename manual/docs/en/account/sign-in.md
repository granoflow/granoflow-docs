Email verification is the main way to sign up or sign in to GranoFlow: enter your email address, then enter the 6-digit code from the email. Supported platforms may also show Google or Apple sign-in. Whichever option you use, it connects to the same GranoFlow account system.

## How to sign in

1. On the sign-in page, choose email, Google, or Apple.
2. If you use email, enter your email address, open your inbox, and find the 6-digit code from GranoFlow.
3. If you use Google or Apple, follow the system or browser prompt to choose an account and authorize it.
4. Return to GranoFlow and wait for sign-in to finish.

Email sign-in has no password, so there is no password to remember or forget.

## Why the same email matters

Sync, device recognition, and subscription benefits are tied to your account. Your account is the email address you use to sign in.

If you sign in with `a@gmail.com` on your phone and `b@gmail.com` on your computer, GranoFlow treats them as **two separate accounts**. Data is not shared between those two accounts.

## What you can do without signing in

You can use GranoFlow without signing in. Tasks, journals, and other local features work normally on this device.

These features require sign-in before they can be used or recognized:

- Cross-device sync
- Subscription recognition
- Encrypted cloud backup

## What signing out does

Signing out **does not delete any data on this device**. Your tasks, journals, and projects remain on this device.

After signing out, you need to sign in again the next time you want to use account-related features.

:::note[Cannot find the email?]
Check your spam folder first. If the verification email still has not arrived after 5 minutes, you can send it again.
:::
