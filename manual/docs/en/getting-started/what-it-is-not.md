GranoFlow has clear boundaries. It is a personal planning app, not an enterprise control surface, not a gamified habit app, and not an AI agent that decides for you.

## Not a task manager built on streaks

GranoFlow is a productivity app without streaks. It does not use daily streak pressure, public rankings, or gamification to keep you returning. Review is for understanding your rhythm, not for proving that you never stopped.

## Not a promise to fix procrastination

GranoFlow can help you build time structure and life structure, but it does not promise to beat procrastination, guarantee productivity, or make every day consistent. It keeps projects, milestones, and review available when real life interrupts the plan.

## Not an AI autopilot

AI-assisted reflection can help organize notes, prompts, and review material. It does not silently change your records or decide your values. Important imports and changes return to user confirmation.

## Not an enterprise admin console

Your private task manager should not become an organizational evaluation tool. GranoFlow is designed for personal growth, private planning, and reflection rather than centralized account inspection or performance reporting.

## Mostly free to use

GranoFlow's local core features are available for long-term free use. You can create tasks, projects, milestones, and daily reviews on your device and build your own time structure. Outside cloud and a small set of personalization features, these everyday planning features do not require a subscription and do not limit the number of tasks, projects, or times you use them.

Paid features mainly unlock a fuller cloud and personalization experience. If you do not need cloud sync or personalization, you do not need to buy a membership.
