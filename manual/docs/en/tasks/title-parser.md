Use #, @, ~, and date hints in the title to organize tasks faster, while knowing what must be confirmed before it becomes a field.

## Where To Start

Type naturally when creating a task or editing its title. `#` hints at tags, `@` hints at projects, `~` hints at reminder time, and date words enter a confirmation state.

## How To Use It

- After typing `#tag` or `@project`, choose a suggestion, press Enter, or press Tab to confirm. Only confirmed choices are written into tag or project fields.
- After typing date or reminder words, check the highlight and suggestion. Click the date suggestion or keep typing after the date with a space to turn it into a structured date.
- Unconfirmed fragments stay in the title and are not silently converted into fields.

## Results And Boundaries

Title parsing reduces organizing effort; it does not decide the task structure for you. You can write the full sentence first, then confirm only the parts that should become structured fields.

- `#`, `@`, and `~` recognition depends on clear boundaries; ordinary text containing those symbols may not become fields.
- Suggestions may be incomplete. The final fields are the ones you confirm.
